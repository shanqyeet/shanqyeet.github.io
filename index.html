About Me

Q1 What’s Your Story?

I usually like to begin with my career experience. Why? Because it’s everywhere (almost). Graduated with Bachelor of Science in Biotech form Monash University, I’ve not engaged in any work related to Biotech honestly.

The first job I had was in Commercial Banking dealing with business loans. Here we like to call them the loan shark with license. Haha. Jokes aside, I’ve really learned a lot from this job because it allowed me to look at how business owners think, work and make decisions, and how businesses manage their cash flow because I had to know all these to convince the bank to loan them the millions that they need to expand or grow their business! This is also when I started to pick up my drinking + socialising skills because it is crucial to connect at a deeper level with our clients to squeeze them dry for cross selling understand their needs well.

Three years into banking, I stumbled upon an opportunity to start my own business! What business was it you ask? It’s a brand of fibre tech cleaning products from Austria that cleans without using any kind of detergent, just water. Being an early adopter, I was totally passionate about it Being young and hot headed, I dived into this business without doing much market research and validation. Not knowing much about business strategy, management and marketing stuff.. I honestly was reading, learning and applying as we go. With that kind of energy I had, I did a satisfactory first year and managed to break even within 9 months! clapping for self. Then… things started to go south and the business was eventually terminated nearly 2 years after we began

I was broke… but no beaten. In 2014, I worked for family business in construction where I learned a lot about project management (and… got certified for it :D) and started to work on my second start-up idea: online marketplace for Healthy, Organic, Natural, Eco, Sustainable & eThical (HONEST) products which was covered by Vulcan Post – HERE!

2 years of design, planning and development, Luveco Marketplace was finally launched in May 2017 and~ terminated in October 2017. Phew… almost suffocated there. Oh I was still seeing a lot of opportunity there but still decided to move because:

  1. I realised that I did not study competition well – or rather understand the landscape of online marketplace and organic products.
  2. Did not do sufficient preparation and was… again literally learning and applying digital marketing, advertising and branding as we go.
  3. Was really losing out on speed due to lack of knowledge, skills and resources.

Once again… I was broke and this situation activated my back up plan - learn coding and pursue a career as programmer. Yeap, I’d attended a three months super intensive full stack web development bootcamp and here I am! Ready, passionate and burning with fire to embark on this new journey!
